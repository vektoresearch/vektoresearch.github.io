About us

Founded in 2021, Vektor Research is an independent research company, which will focus on research and advisory service. The big idea comes from the need of independent opinions and a research institution that promotes independency. We realize that there are so many, or perhaps too many, stocks to cover, yet manpower to cover stocks is indeed limited.

We will focus on long-tail strategy, while we also plan to introduce alternative-data-powered research to complement your research. Do you need us to solve your problems? Or perhaps, do you need a specifically-tailored research to suit your needs? We plan to assist you in the near future.

Our team plans to provide you with other independent opinions from direct or podcast-type interviews, which, we hope, will help you gain more insights.
