About us

Founded in 2021, Vektor Research is an independent research company, which will focus on research publications and on-demand service. The big idea comes from the need of independent opinions and a research institution that promotes independency.

We will focus on long-tail strategy, while we also plan to introduce alternative-data-powered research to complement your research. Do you need us to solve your problems? Or perhaps, do you need a specifically-tailored research to suit your needs? We plan to assist you in the near future.

Our team plans to provide you with other independent opinions from direct or podcast-type interviews, which, we hope, will help you gain more insights to help make sound investment decisions.

For now, we regularly publish our thoughts for Seeking Alpha.
